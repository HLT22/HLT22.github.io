A little about me

My name is Hunter Thurmond and I love Math! The thing I love most about math is learning about profound facts of nature. Two of my favorite examples are Varignon's Polygon and The Painter's Paradox of Gabriel's Horn. Math is the only way to experience and see such things! A love for Math goes hand in hand with a love of nature. When I'm not coding(which is Math :]) I am probably with my wife fossil hunting, bird watching, star gazing or enterntaining ourselves with houseplants.
